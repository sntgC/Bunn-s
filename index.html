About Us

A family enterntainment center based in Santa Cruz, CA.

Here at Bunn's, we strive to combine enterntainment for all ages at a modest price. Featuring immersive virtual reality, action-packed laser tag, multi-lane bowling alleys, and hundreds of arcade games, you'll never run out of things to do. We serve a mix of American fare influenced by Caribbean and Hispanic dishes and local cuisine, and offer a beverage station with thousands of possibilities. Whether you are eight or eighty, Bunn's has something for everyone.
